# Plan 10: Zombie School — Oddbods Teachers

**Target**: Ages 5-7 | **Theme**: School / Classroom | **Characters**: Alphabet + Zombies + Oddbods

## Story
Oddbods are teachers at a school for zombies! The zombie students need to learn their ABCs to graduate. Help teach them!

## Mechanics
- A zombie student stands at a chalkboard
- The chalkboard shows a word with a missing letter
- Letters float around the classroom
- Pop the correct letter to complete the word
- Correct = zombie writes the letter on the board and cheers
- Wrong = zombie erases the board with a funny face and tries again
- Each lesson focuses on one letter of the alphabet

## Oddbods as Teachers (one per subject)
- Bubbles (Science Teacher): Explains letter sounds with experiments. "A is for Apple! Let's grow one!"
- Jeff (Math/Organization Teacher): Teaches alphabet order. "A comes before B. B comes before C."
- Newt (Art Teacher): Draws the letter with glitter and colors. "Let's make A beautiful!"
- Fuse (Gym Teacher): Makes letter shapes with their body. "Stretch your arms for A!"
- Pogo (Recess Teacher): Turns lessons into games. "Who can find the letter A fastest?"
- Slick (Music Teacher): Sings the alphabet song. "Let's sing it together!"
- Zee (Nap Time/Nature): Quiet letter tracing while everyone rests.

## Zombie Students (7 types)
- Classic: Eager student, raises hand for every question
- Decayed: Daydreamer — stares out window, needs prompting
- Toxic: Messy desk — letters everywhere, hard to focus
- Undead: Night class student — sleepy but tries hard
- Rotten: Clumsy — drops chalk, trips over desk
- Ghoul: Class clown — makes silly faces, everyone laughs
- Mutant: Overachiever — already knows some letters

## Classroom Setup
- Desks in rows with zombie students
- Chalkboard at front with the word + blank
- Teacher's desk with the Oddbod teacher
- Alphabet chart on the wall (fills as letters are learned)
- Letter blocks scattered on floor as floating collectibles

## Progression
- 26 lessons (one per letter)
- Each lesson: 3 words featuring that letter
- All 3 correct = letter is "learned" and added to the wall chart
- After every 5 letters learned = recess break (mini-game)

## Recess Mini-Games
- Pogo leads a letter race (pop letters in order)
- Bubbles does a science demo (letter volcano)
- Slick organizes a letter dance party

## Graduation
- After all 26 letters learned = Graduation Ceremony
- All 7 zombie students in tiny graduation caps and gowns
- Oddbods hand out diplomas shaped like letters
- Confetti and celebration

## Scoring
- Each correct letter = star (1-3 based on speed)
- Full lesson (3 words) = lesson complete badge
- Speed bonus for fast answers

## Win Condition
- Complete all 26 lessons
- Graduate the zombie class
- No lose condition — every lesson is passable
