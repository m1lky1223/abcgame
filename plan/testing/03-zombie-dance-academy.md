# Plan 03: Zombie Dance Academy

**Target**: Ages 5-7 | **Theme**: Dance / Music | **Characters**: Alphabet + Zombies + Oddbods

## Story
Zombies want to learn their ABCs through dance! YOU are the dance teacher. Oddbods are the judges.

## Mechanics
- A zombie student stands on a dance floor
- A word with a missing letter appears on the disco ball above
- Letters float as musical notes/dance moves
- Pop the correct letter to teach the zombie
- Correct = zombie does a victory dance unique to its type
- Wrong = zombie shrugs with a silly face, music skips, try again

## Zombie Dance Styles (7 unique dances)
- Classic: The Twist (wiggle side to side)
- Decayed: The Robot (jerky mechanical moves)
- Toxic: The Worm (green slime trail)
- Undead: The Moonwalk (slides backward)
- Rotten: The Floss (modern dance)
- Ghoul: The Monster Mash (stomp stomp clap)
- Mutant: The Breakdance (spins on floor)

## Oddbods Dance Judges
- Slick (Head Judge): Rates moves 1-3 stars, wears sunglasses
- Pogo: Laughs at everything, gives bonus points for funny
- Bubbles: Scientific analysis — "Excellent letter form!"
- Newt: Heart eyes when zombie dances well
- Fuse: Stamps feet to the beat
- Jeff: Judges based on letter accuracy
- Zee: Wakes up only for perfect scores, then claps once

## Scoring
- Correct letter = zombie learns it + dance move unlocked
- Each zombie learns 4 letters (7 zombies × 4 = 28 rounds)
- After all 4 letters = zombie performs full dance routine
- Stars awarded per performance

## Win Condition
- All 7 zombies have learned their 4-letter routines
- Grand Finale: All 7 zombies + all 7 Oddbods dance together
- Screen fills with dancing characters and confetti

## Visual Style
- Disco ball with sparkle reflections
- Dance floor with colored tiles that light up
- Musical notes float as particles
- Spotlight follows each zombie performance

## Code Reuse
- ZombieChaser.ts (all 7 zombie designs, reused as dancers)
- FloatingLetter.ts (musical note letters that float)
- Word pop logic
- Celebration particles

## New Code Needed
- Dance animation per zombie type (different movement patterns)
- Disco ball rendering (rotating sphere with light reflections)
- Dance floor tile grid
- Music note particle system
- Judge reaction panel (Oddbod close-up with speech bubble)
- Star rating screen per performance

## Kid-Friendly Notes
- Music and dancing = universally fun for 5-7
- No wrong-answer penalty — just a shrug and retry
- Each zombie has a distinct, funny dance to watch
- Dance themes encourage physical movement (kids may dance along!)
- "Teaching" gives a sense of pride and accomplishment
